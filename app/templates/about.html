About

The purpose of this project is to provide the forecast of Heat Quantity at four different countries (United States, Belgium, Russian Federation, Japan) based on the heat data between 1995 - 2009. Data is downloaded from UN database (reference on Github). A regression model is implmented, trained, and deployed to a web application together with visualization.

Heat Prediction Explained

What is Heat Quantity you may ask?

Well, Heat Quantity is the heat decipated from any heat-related activities: Consumption of energy, Melting objects, Burning fossil fuel, you name it all. And, is Measured in Terajoules (10^12).

Is this concerning?

As a rising energy consumption is closely related to a rising population, we are getting nearer and nearer, to a tipping point or an energy crisis.

As demand rises indefinitely, the quantity of natural resources deminishes.
